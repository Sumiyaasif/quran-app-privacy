# Privacy Policy

This application is provided to offer users a respectful and simple way to read and listen to the Holy Qur’an.

## Data Collection
This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data. No user accounts, profiles, or identifiable information are required to use the app.

## Permissions
The app may request standard permissions such as internet access and audio playback in order to provide core functionality, including loading content and playing recitations. These permissions are used only to support app features and are not used to collect user data.

## Third-Party Services
This app does not use third-party analytics, advertising services, or tracking tools.

## Children’s Privacy
This app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy
This privacy policy may be updated if the app’s functionality changes. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
